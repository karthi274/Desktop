B.TECH CSBS , K.KARTHIK PRABU

Our 4-year B.Tech in Computer Science and Business Systems programme focuses on imparting the necessary skills and knowledge to create future technology. The programme covers all the underlying aspects of modern computer science, along with the theory and practical knowledge needed to build a successful and impactful career in the field of information technology, software development, and computer science. A B.Tech in Computer Science and Business Systems from our institute covers a well-designed curriculum that comprises experiential learning to meet the changing needs of the computer science industry. The programme aims to create an excellent academic ambience that promotes innovation and research, along with the collaboration of world-class academic institutions and software industries for mutual benefit. A degree in this programme will cover the following topics: Fundamentals of Computer Science and STEM Software Engineering and Statistical Studies Design Analysis and Analytical Thinking Computer Networks & Information Security Usability Design and It Project Management

The programme will assist students in identifying, formulating and solving engineering problems to provide efficient solutions. Upon the completion of the programme, Graduates will be able to design and develop computer-based applications of varying complexities in emerging areas of Computer Science and Engineering. Graduates can apply the skills learnt through internships and collaborative projects with the industry in real-time project development.
